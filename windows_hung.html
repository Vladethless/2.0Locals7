What is a Double-Hung Window?

A double-hung window contains two frames stacked on top of each other, with both frames opening independently to let air in or keep the heat out. The sections use weights to stay open, so you don't need anything else to hold them open or closed. They come in several different styles: sash/swing (pictured), picture, and casement/slider.
