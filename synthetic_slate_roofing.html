Hire Quality Synthetic Slate Roofing Experts

Synthetic slate roofing is a beautiful choice for homeowners looking to make their homes look more traditional. Slate is not only attractive but can last for hundreds of years without any major repairs. On top of being aesthetically pleasing, slate roofs are also very durable. Slate is one of the most durable materials used in construction, second only to concrete, with slates lasting up to 200 years with proper maintenance.

Why Synthetic Slate Roofing is a Great Option?

Slate roofs are sustainable because they are made from natural stone and recycled copper, which means they don't require any special chemicals or compounds for protection against the elements. Slate roofs are also made with less waste compared to other types of roofing. The materials are cut out of large pieces of slate, which are then used for the next 20-30 years of the roof's lifetime. These slates can be recycled into many different things after they have been used on a roof for the first time.

Slate is also easier to install than other types of roofing materials due to its flat nature. At first, slate was not an option because it was considered too heavy to layover older houses, but now it is used more often due to its durability and ease of installation.

Available Options

Several different types of slate roofs can be used for different purposes combined with the proper installation. Synthetic slates usually come in thin layers of felt directly attached to a plastic membrane to prevent leaks. They are best suited for newer homes where structural integrity is vital to avoid leaking.
