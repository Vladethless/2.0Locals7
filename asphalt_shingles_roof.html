You can easily find a shingle that looks exactly like the rest of your neighborhood and will give a very subtle appearance to your house. Also, these roofs are made to last. Asphalt shingles are sold at many retailers, and you can be sure that they will last up to 20 years, owing to their durability. It is worth noting that if you decide to go with Asphalt shingles, the quality and lifespan of your new roof will be better.

Types of Asphalt Shingle Roofs

There are two kinds of asphalt shingles:

  • Organic
  • Fiberglass

Organic shingles look similar to wood; Thave a layered design and come in several colors:

Fiberglass shingles look like traditional squares-they're very straightforward, but they do come in several colors as well.
